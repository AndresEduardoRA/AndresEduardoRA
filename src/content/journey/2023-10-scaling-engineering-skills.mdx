## What I'm Learning

**Performance matters**: Understanding how to optimize queries, reduce load times, and handle scale is important.

**Security is complex**: There are many layers to security, from authentication to data protection to preventing common vulnerabilities.

**Architecture patterns exist for a reason**: Patterns like MVC, microservices, and event-driven architecture solve specific problems.
I use AI assistants to compare architectural options and outline trade‑off matrices before I make decisions.
